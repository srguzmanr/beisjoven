1.1 · Vision
Mission
Somos el futuro del beis.

Beisjoven is the Spanish-language editorial home for the next generation of Mexican baseball and softball — built for readers who take the sport seriously and want coverage that does too. We exist because no other publication is treating youth, college, LMB, MLB, Selección and softbol as one continuous story, with one editorial voice.

What this means in practice

Every editorial decision — every headline, every photo crop, every pillar label — should read as if written by a publication that already exists in 2030 and has been setting the terms of the conversation for years. We are a paper of record for a beat that doesn't yet have one.

1.3 · Classification rules · authoritative

When pillars collide — how we file

A story almost never belongs to two pillars. When it looks like it does, one of these rules resolves it. Rule 1 and Rule 2 take precedence over everything.

  1. El deporte manda sobre la edad

    Cualquier historia de sofbol — juvenil, universitario, profesional o de Selección — se publica bajo Softbol. Nunca bajo Juvenil.

    Femenil Sub-18 vs Japón → Softbol

  2. La institución MLB manda sobre la edad

    Programas propiedad u operados por MLB (Academy, DSL, MLB Prospect League) se publican bajo MLB, aunque los peloteros sean menores de 18. La marca institucional prevalece sobre el corte demográfico.

    MLB Academy Monterrey → MLB (no Juvenil)

  3. WBSC (selecciones internacionales) → Selección

    Cualquier evento bajo paraguas WBSC con selección nacional mexicana participando: Serie del Caribe, Premier12, Panamericanos, Olimpiadas.

    Mexico Rojo vs Venezuela (Serie del Caribe) → Selección

  4. WBC (Clásico Mundial) → MLB

    Excepción a la regla WBSC. El Clásico Mundial es operado por MLB y se clasifica bajo MLB, aunque compitan selecciones nacionales.

    México vs EE.UU. Clásico 2026 → MLB

  5. BCL Americas → Ligas MX

    Baseball Champions League Americas se publica bajo Ligas, no Selección, porque los participantes son clubes profesionales (no selecciones nacionales).

    Diablos en BCL Americas → Ligas MX

  6. College baseball (DI) → Juvenil

    Peloteros mexicanos en NCAA Division I se publican bajo Juvenil. Es formación — el siguiente escalón después de Sub-18.

    Mexicanos en DI 2026 → Juvenil

  7. College softball → Softbol

    Regla 1 prevalece. Sofbol universitario se publica bajo Softbol, nunca Juvenil.

    Sofbol Universitario USA 2026 → Softbol
1.4 · Byline

One voice, one byline

All reporting and straight news is attributed to Redacción Beisjoven. The operator's personal name never appears as a byline in Phase 1. Opinion columns may carry a personal byline once Phase 2 opens (TBD).

✓ Correct
Por Redacción Beisjoven · 19 abr 2026 · 4 min
✗ Never
Por [operator personal name] · 19 abr 2026 · 4 min
Why

One byline builds institutional voice. Three readers finding the same byline in three weeks start perceiving Beisjoven as a paper, not a blog. It also insulates the operator from attribution-level risk during Phase 1.

2.8 · Accessibility floors · WCAG AA

Accessibility

Minimum 4.5:1 contrast for body text, 3:1 for large text (≥24px or ≥19px bold). One color combination in our palette fails — it's called out below and never used as body text.

Never as body

2.67:1. Gold fails on white — never body text. Use only as accent rule, focus ring (outline, not text), or on navy bg.

Other floors

All tap targets ≥ 44×44px (iOS HIG). Focus-visible always shows — never suppressed. Prefers-reduced-motion respected on the Quick Hits ticker and LIVE pulse. Language attribute lang="es" on every page.

Four templates — Homepage, Article, Category, Author. Module order is authoritative. Components are referenced by name; their anatomy lives in Part 2.

3.0 · Header · shared across all templates

Header anatomy

Every template starts with the same header stack. Quick Hits is always-on; LIVE strip is conditional and sits above Quick Hits when active. Mobile auto-hides at 80px scroll; desktop shrinks to the nav row on sticky.

1. Brand row
Mark-only mobile · Mark + Wordmark desktop · 44×44 tap targets
2. Nav
Mobile: horizontal scrollable chips with right-edge fade gradient · Desktop: inline category list with 2px underline on active (category-accent color)
3. Quick Hits
Admin-editable, always-present, rotates every 4.5s, 36px tall
4. LIVE strip
Conditional — event-only. Sits above Quick Hits. Red EN VIVO pill + pulse, category-color tab
Search
Icon-only 44×44, aria-label="Buscar". Tap opens full-viewport overlay
Scroll behavior
Mobile: auto-hide at 80px scroll-down, reveal on scroll-up · Desktop: shrinks to nav-only on sticky
